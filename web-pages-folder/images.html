JPG is used with pictures with lots of gradients in them and they do not allow transparent pixels.

More formats

GIF's are good option in simple animations and they can be tremendously useful to communicate, sometimes being better than still images.

PNG do deal with opacity and don't have color palette limitations. Excellent fit for logos, icons or technical diagrams.

SVG is a vector-based graphic format, that means it can scale up or down to any dimension without losing quality.
